Itinerary

Day1

Arrival in Goa – Relax at Arambol Beach

Arrive at Goa Airport/Railway Station, where our representative will greet you. Transfer to your hotel in North Goa. Spend the evening at Arambol Beach, a peaceful paradise known for its scenic cliffs, vibrant beach shacks, and sunset drum circles. Stay overnight in North Goa.

Day2

North Goa Sightseeing – Beaches & Forts

After breakfast, visit Aguada Fort, a historic Portuguese fort offering stunning sea views. Explore Calangute Beach, Baga Beach, and Anjuna Beach, known for their lively atmosphere and water sports. End the day at Vagator Beach, famous for its dramatic red cliffs. Stay overnight in North Goa.

Day3

Water Sports & Nightlife Experience

Enjoy a thrilling day with water sports like parasailing, jet skiing, and banana boat rides at Calangute Beach. In the evening, experience Goa’s vibrant nightlife at Tito’s Lane or visit a beach club for live music and entertainment. Stay overnight in North Goa.

Day4

South Goa – Churches & Sunset Cruise

Check out from your hotel and head to South Goa. Visit the famous Basilica of Bom Jesus and Se Cathedral, both UNESCO-listed churches. Later, relax at Colva Beach before enjoying a sunset cruise on the Mandovi River. Stay overnight in South Goa.

Day5

Explore Palolem & Butterfly Beach

After breakfast, visit Palolem Beach, one of Goa’s most scenic beaches with golden sand and a relaxed vibe. Take a boat ride to Butterfly Beach, a secluded paradise with crystal-clear waters. Stay overnight in South Goa.

Day6

Dudhsagar Waterfalls & Spice Plantation Tour

Embark on an exciting day trip to Dudhsagar Waterfalls, one of India’s tallest waterfalls. En route, visit a spice plantation, where you can learn about Goa’s rich spice culture. Stay overnight in South Goa.

Day7

Leisure & Shopping at Flea Markets

Spend your day shopping at the Anjuna Flea Market or Mapusa Market, where you can buy souvenirs, handicrafts, and beachwear. Enjoy a relaxing spa session or unwind at your favorite beach. Stay overnight in Goa.

Day8

Departure from Goa

After breakfast, check out and transfer to Goa Airport/Railway Station for your return journey, concluding your beach getaway.
