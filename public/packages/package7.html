Itinerary

Day1

Arrival in Gangtok

Arrive at Bagdogra Airport or NJP Railway Station, where our representative will greet you. Drive to Gangtok, the capital of Sikkim. Check into your hotel and spend the evening at MG Marg, exploring local markets and trying authentic Sikkimese cuisine. Stay overnight in Gangtok.

Day2

Tsomgo Lake & Baba Mandir Excursion

After breakfast, drive to the breathtaking Tsomgo Lake, a glacial lake surrounded by snow-clad mountains. Visit Baba Harbhajan Singh Mandir, a revered shrine. If time permits, visit Nathula Pass (subject to permit availability) for a mesmerizing experience at the Indo-China border. Return to Gangtok for an overnight stay.

Day3

Gangtok to Lachen (120 km / 6 hrs)

After breakfast, head towards Lachen, a picturesque village in North Sikkim. En route, visit the stunning Seven Sisters Waterfalls, Singhik Viewpoint, and Naga Waterfalls. Arrive in Lachen, check into your hotel, and enjoy the serene mountain atmosphere before an overnight stay.

Day4

Gurudongmar Lake Excursion – Transfer to Lachung

Wake up early and drive to Gurudongmar Lake, one of the highest lakes in the world, offering breathtaking views of the snow-covered mountains. Return to Lachen for lunch and proceed to Lachung, another beautiful valley town. Check into your hotel and relax. Stay overnight in Lachung.

Day5

Yumthang Valley & Zero Point Excursion – Return to Gangtok

After breakfast, visit Yumthang Valley, known as the 'Valley of Flowers,' offering stunning alpine landscapes and hot springs. If accessible, visit Zero Point, where you can witness snow even in summer. Return to Gangtok in the evening for an overnight stay.

Day6

Gangtok to Pelling (120 km / 5 hrs)

After breakfast, drive to Pelling, a serene hill town known for its mesmerizing views of Mount Kanchenjunga. Check into your hotel and visit Pemayangtse Monastery and Rabdentse Ruins, offering a glimpse of Sikkim’s rich history. Overnight stay in Pelling.

Day7

Pelling Sightseeing – Return to Gangtok

Explore Kanchenjunga Waterfalls, Khecheopalri Lake, and Skywalk. Enjoy the breathtaking views before heading back to Gangtok. Spend the evening at leisure before an overnight stay.

Day8

Departure from Gangtok

Enjoy a peaceful morning in Auli before heading back to Dehradun for your departure, concluding your memorable Uttarakhand journey.
