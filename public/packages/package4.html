Kerala Tour

Experience the enchanting beauty of Kerala - Wander through Kochi’s heritage streets, admire Munnar’s tea gardens, and spot wildlife in Thekkady. Cruise through Alleppey’s backwaters on a houseboat and relax in Kumarakom. Enjoy Kovalam’s pristine beaches, witness the stunning sunset at Kanyakumari, and explore Trivandrum’s cultural gems. This trip blends nature, adventure, and relaxation for a perfect getaway.

Itinerary

Day1

Arrival in Kochi

Arrive in Kochi and transfer to your hotel. Explore Fort Kochi, Jew Town, St. Francis Church, and the iconic Chinese Fishing Nets. In the evening, enjoy a Kathakali dance performance before an overnight stay.

Day2

Kochi – Munnar (130 km / 4 hrs)

Drive to Munnar, stopping at Cheeyappara and Valara Waterfalls. Check into your hotel and relax amidst the tea plantations.

Day3

Munnar Sightseeing

Visit Eravikulam National Park, Mattupetty Dam, Echo Point, and Top Station. Explore tea plantations and the Tea Museum before returning to your hotel.

Day4

Munnar – Thekkady (90 km / 3 hrs)

Travel to Thekkady and enjoy a boat safari in Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary. Visit spice plantations and watch an optional Kalaripayattu martial arts show.

Day5

Thekkady – Alleppey (140 km / 4 hrs)

Head to Alleppey and check into a houseboat. Cruise through serene backwaters, enjoy traditional Kerala meals, and stay overnight on the houseboat.

Day6

Alleppey – Kumarakom (30 km / 1 hr)

Disembark and drive to Kumarakom. Visit Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary and relax by the backwaters or enjoy an Ayurvedic spa.

Day7

Kumarakom – Kovalam (180 km / 5 hrs)

Travel to Kovalam and explore Lighthouse Beach, Hawa Beach, and Samudra Beach. Enjoy a sunset view and an overnight stay.

Day8

Kovalam – Kanyakumari (90 km / 2.5 hrs)

Visit Kanyakumari, explore Vivekananda Rock Memorial, Thiruvalluvar Statue, and Kanyakumari Temple. Watch the sunset before returning to the hotel.

Day9

Kanyakumari – Trivandrum (90 km / 2.5 hrs)

Drive to Trivandrum, visit Padmanabhaswamy Temple, Napier Museum, and Shanghumukham Beach.

Day10

Leisure Day in Trivandrum

Enjoy a leisure day or visit Varkala Beach. Indulge in shopping, seafood, or an Ayurvedic massage.

Day11

Departure from Trivandrum

Depart from Trivandrum Airport, concluding your memorable Kerala trip.
