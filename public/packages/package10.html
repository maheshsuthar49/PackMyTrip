Itinerary

Day1

Arrival in Chennai – Sightseeing

Arrive at Chennai International Airport or Railway Station, where our representative will greet you. Visit Marina Beach, Kapaleeshwarar Temple, Fort St. George, and Santhome Cathedral before an overnight stay in Chennai.

Day2

Chennai to Mahabalipuram (60 km / 2 hrs) – Sightseeing

After breakfast, drive to Mahabalipuram, famous for its UNESCO-listed rock-cut temples. Visit Shore Temple, Arjuna’s Penance, and the Five Rathas. Enjoy the beach before staying overnight in Mahabalipuram.

Day3

Mahabalipuram to Pondicherry (100 km / 3 hrs)

Proceed to Pondicherry, a former French colony. Explore Aurobindo Ashram, Auroville, Rock Beach, and French Quarters. Enjoy the colonial charm before staying overnight in Pondicherry.

Day4

Pondicherry to Thanjavur (165 km / 4 hrs)

After breakfast, drive to Thanjavur, known for its magnificent temples. Visit Brihadeeswarar Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage site, and explore the Thanjavur Palace and Art Gallery before an overnight stay.

Day5

Thanjavur to Madurai (190 km / 5 hrs) – Sightseeing

Proceed to Madurai, the cultural capital of Tamil Nadu. Visit the famous Meenakshi Amman Temple, Thirumalai Nayakkar Mahal, and the Gandhi Memorial Museum before staying overnight in Madurai.

Day6

Madurai to Rameswaram (170 km / 3.5 hrs)

Travel to Rameswaram, a sacred Hindu pilgrimage site. Visit Ramanathaswamy Temple, Pamban Bridge, Dhanushkodi, and Agni Theertham before an overnight stay.

Day7

Rameswaram to Kanyakumari (310 km / 6 hrs)

Drive to Kanyakumari, the southernmost tip of India. Visit Vivekananda Rock Memorial, Thiruvalluvar Statue, Kanyakumari Temple, and witness the breathtaking sunset before staying overnight.

Day8

Kanyakumari to Kodaikanal (330 km / 7 hrs)

Head to Kodaikanal, a beautiful hill station. Visit Coaker’s Walk, Bryant Park, Silver Cascade Falls, and Kodai Lake before staying overnight.

Day9

Kodaikanal to Ooty (250 km / 6 hrs)

Proceed to Ooty, the 'Queen of Hill Stations.' Visit Ooty Lake, Botanical Gardens, Doddabetta Peak, and Tea Museum before an overnight stay in Ooty.

Day10

Ooty to Coimbatore (85 km / 3 hrs) – Departure

After breakfast, check out and proceed to Coimbatore for your onward journey, concluding your Tamil Nadu adventure.
