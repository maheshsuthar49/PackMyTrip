Itinerary

Day1

Arrival in Manali – Relax & Explore

Arrive in Manali via bus or private transport, where our representative will greet you. Check in to your hotel and relax. In the evening, explore Mall Road, shop for local handicrafts, and enjoy café hopping. Overnight stay in Manali.

Day2

Solang Valley – Adventure & Snow Activities

After breakfast, head to Solang Valley, famous for adventure sports. Enjoy paragliding, skiing, snow scooter rides, and a cable car ride. If visiting in winter, experience the beautiful snow-covered landscapes. Return to Manali for an overnight stay.

Day3

Rohtang Pass / Atal Tunnel Excursion (Optional)

Early morning, take an excursion to Rohtang Pass (subject to weather & permit availability). If Rohtang is closed, visit Atal Tunnel & Sissu Valley, offering mesmerizing views of the Himalayas. Spend time in the snow-covered meadows before returning to Manali.

Day4

Local Sightseeing – Temples & Waterfalls

Visit Hadimba Devi Temple, known for its unique wooden architecture. Explore Manu Temple, Vashisht Hot Water Springs, and Jogini Waterfall. Enjoy a relaxing evening by the Beas River. Overnight stay in Manali.

Day5

Kullu & Naggar Castle

After breakfast, take a scenic drive to Kullu, where you can enjoy river rafting on the Beas River. Visit the famous Naggar Castle, an ancient heritage site with stunning valley views. Explore local art at Nicholas Roerich Art Gallery before returning to Manali.

Day6

Departure from Manali

After breakfast, check out from the hotel. Explore Manali at your leisure before departing for Delhi/Chandigarh via Volvo or private transport, marking the end of your memorable Himalayan getaway.
