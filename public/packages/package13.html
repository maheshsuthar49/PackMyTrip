Itinerary

Day1

Arrival in Agatti Island – Beach Relaxation

Arrive at Agatti Airport, where our representative will welcome you. Transfer to your beachside resort. Spend the evening unwinding at Agatti Beach, known for its serene shores and breathtaking sunset views. Stay overnight in Agatti.

Day2

Water Sports & Island Exploration

After breakfast, enjoy thrilling water sports like snorkeling, kayaking, and jet skiing in the calm waters of Agatti. Later, explore the local village and learn about Lakshadweep’s unique culture. Stay overnight in Agatti.

Day3

Excursion to Bangaram Island – Coral Paradise

Take a speedboat to Bangaram Island, an uninhabited paradise with crystal-clear waters and rich marine life. Enjoy scuba diving or simply relax on the pristine beach. Return to Agatti for an overnight stay.

Day4

Visit Kavaratti Island – Marine Museum & Dolphin Watching

After breakfast, take a boat ride to Kavaratti Island, the capital of Lakshadweep. Visit the Marine Museum and enjoy a dolphin-watching excursion in the open sea. Spend some time at the beautiful lagoons before heading back to Agatti. Stay overnight.

Day5

Explore Kalpeni Island – Lagoon Walk & Leisure Time

Visit Kalpeni Island, famous for its large lagoon and scenic coral atolls. Take a lagoon walk, explore the breathtaking landscapes, or simply enjoy a lazy day by the beach. Return to Agatti for the night.

Day6

Departure from Agatti

After breakfast, check out and transfer to Agatti Airport for your return journey, concluding an unforgettable island escape.
