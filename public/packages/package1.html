Itinerary

Day1

Arrival in Jaipur – Explore the Pink City

Arrive at Jaipur Airport/Railway Station, where our representative will greet you. Transfer to your hotel and relax. In the evening, visit Birla Temple and Nahargarh Fort for a stunning sunset view. Stroll through Johri Bazaar before an overnight stay in Jaipur.

Day2

Jaipur Sightseeing – Forts & Palaces

After breakfast, visit Amber Fort, where you can enjoy an elephant ride. Explore Jaigarh Fort and Nahargarh Fort before visiting Hawa Mahal, Jantar Mantar, and City Palace. In the evening, experience Rajasthani culture at Chokhi Dhani with traditional dance and cuisine. Overnight stay in Jaipur.

Day3

Jaipur to Bikaner – The Desert Town

Drive to Bikaner after breakfast. Visit Junagarh Fort, Lalgarh Palace, and the famous Karni Mata Temple (Rat Temple). Enjoy a traditional Rajasthani dinner. Overnight stay in Bikaner.

Day4

Bikaner to Jaisalmer – The Golden City

After breakfast, head towards Jaisalmer. Visit Gadisar Lake and enjoy an evening exploring local markets for handicrafts and Rajasthani jewelry. Overnight stay in Jaisalmer.

Day5

Jaisalmer Sightseeing & Desert Safari

Explore Jaisalmer Fort, Patwon Ki Haveli, and Salim Singh Ki Haveli. In the afternoon, proceed to Sam Sand Dunes for a thrilling desert safari, camel ride, and cultural night with folk dance and music. Stay overnight in a desert camp.

Day6

Jaisalmer to Jodhpur – The Blue City

After breakfast, drive to Jodhpur. Visit the majestic Mehrangarh Fort, Jaswant Thada, and the royal Umaid Bhawan Palace. Explore the bustling Clock Tower Market in the evening. Overnight stay in Jodhpur.

Day7

Jodhpur to Mount Abu – Rajasthan’s Only Hill Station

After breakfast, drive to Mount Abu. Visit Nakki Lake, Guru Shikhar, and Dilwara Jain Temples. Enjoy a peaceful sunset at Sunset Point. Overnight stay in Mount Abu.

Day8

Mount Abu to Udaipur – The City of Lakes

After breakfast, head to Udaipur. Enjoy a boat ride on Lake Pichola, visiting Jag Mandir and the Lake Palace. Overnight stay in Udaipur.

Day9

Udaipur Sightseeing – Heritage & Royalty

Explore City Palace, Jagdish Temple, Saheliyon Ki Bari, and Fateh Sagar Lake. Visit Bagore Ki Haveli for a cultural dance show in the evening. Overnight stay in Udaipur.

Day10

Udaipur to Pushkar – A Spiritual Retreat

Drive to Pushkar, a holy town famous for Pushkar Lake and Brahma Temple. Explore the vibrant local market before relaxing in a peaceful desert setting. Overnight stay in Pushkar.

Day11

Departure from Jaipur

After breakfast, return to Jaipur for your onward journey, concluding your Rajasthan adventure.
