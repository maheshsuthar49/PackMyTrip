Meghalaya & Assam Tour

Itinerary

Day1

Arrival in Guwahati – Transfer to Shillong

Arrive at Guwahati Airport, where our representative will receive you. Transfer to Shillong, stopping at Umiam Lake en route. Enjoy an evening stroll at Police Bazaar before an overnight stay in Shillong.

Day2

Excursion to Cherrapunji – Waterfalls & Caves

After breakfast, head to Cherrapunji, one of the wettest places on Earth. Explore Nohkalikai Falls, Seven Sisters Falls, Mawsmai Cave, and Arwah Cave. Return to Shillong for an overnight stay.

Day3

Mawlynnong & Dawki – Asia’s Cleanest Village & Umngot River

Visit Mawlynnong, known as the cleanest village in Asia, and explore its living root bridges. Proceed to Dawki for a mesmerizing boat ride on the crystal-clear Umngot River. Return to Shillong for an overnight stay.

Day4

Shillong Sightseeing & Transfer to Kaziranga

Explore Shillong attractions, including Shillong Peak, Elephant Falls, and Don Bosco Museum. Later, drive to Kaziranga National Park and stay overnight at a jungle resort.

Day5

Kaziranga Safari & Transfer to Guwahati

Enjoy an early morning jeep safari in Kaziranga to spot the famous one-horned rhinoceros. After breakfast, transfer to Guwahati and visit the revered Kamakhya Temple. Stay overnight in Guwahati.

Day6

Sualkuchi & Hajo – Assam’s Cultural Gems

Explore Sualkuchi, Assam’s silk-weaving village, followed by a visit to Hajo, an ancient pilgrimage site. Stay overnight in Guwahati.

Day7

Brahmaputra River Cruise & Local Shopping

Enjoy a sunset cruise on the Brahmaputra River and explore Guwahati’s vibrant local markets for souvenirs. Stay overnight in Guwahati.

Day8

Departure from Guwahati

After breakfast, transfer to Guwahati Airport for your departure, concluding a memorable Northeast India tour.
