Uttarakhand Tour

Experience the mesmerizing beauty of Uttarakhand - Explore the picturesque hill stations of Nainital and Mussoorie, the spiritual aura of Haridwar and Rishikesh, the breathtaking landscapes of Auli, and the wildlife adventure in Jim Corbett. Itinerary

Day1

Arrival in Dehradun – Transfer to Mussoorie

Arrive at Dehradun Airport or Railway Station, where our representative will greet you. Drive to Mussoorie, known as the ‘Queen of Hills.’ Check into your hotel and spend the evening at Mall Road or visit Gun Hill for a panoramic view of the Himalayas. Enjoy an overnight stay in Mussoorie.

Day2

Mussoorie Sightseeing

After breakfast, explore Kempty Falls, Lal Tibba, and Company Garden. Visit George Everest House for stunning sunset views. Enjoy leisure time shopping before returning to your hotel for an overnight stay.

Day3

Mussoorie to Rishikesh – Haridwar (90 km / 3 hrs)

Depart for Rishikesh, the ‘Yoga Capital of the World.’ Visit Ram Jhula, Laxman Jhula, and Triveni Ghat. Later, drive to Haridwar and attend the Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri in the evening. Stay overnight in Haridwar.

Day4

Haridwar to Jim Corbett (120 km / 4 hrs)

After breakfast, drive to Jim Corbett National Park, India’s oldest wildlife sanctuary. Check into your resort and relax. In the evening, visit Garjia Devi Temple and Corbett Museum before an overnight stay at the resort.

Day5

Jim Corbett to Nainital (90 km / 3 hrs)

Wake up early for a thrilling jeep safari in Corbett National Park, spotting tigers, elephants, and exotic birds. After breakfast, head to Nainital, the ‘City of Lakes.’ Check into your hotel and enjoy a relaxing boat ride on Naini Lake before exploring Mall Road. Overnight stay in Nainital.

Day6

Nainital Sightseeing

Visit Snow View Point, Naina Devi Temple, Tiffin Top, and Bhimtal. Explore the beautiful Sattal and Naukuchiatal lakes before an evening at leisure. Stay overnight in Nainital.

Day7

Nainital to Auli (280 km / 8 hrs)

After breakfast, depart for Auli, a stunning hill station known for skiing and cable cars. Enjoy a scenic ropeway ride offering breathtaking views of the Himalayas. Check into your resort and relax amid the snowy peaks. Overnight stay in Auli.

Day8

Departure from Dehradun

Enjoy a peaceful morning in Auli before heading back to Dehradun for your departure, concluding your memorable Uttarakhand journey.
