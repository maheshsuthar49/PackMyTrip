Kashmir Tour

Experience the breathtaking beauty of Kashmir – "The Paradise on Earth" with this 7 Nights / 8 Days tour. Itinerary

Day1

Arrival in Srinagar – Shikara Ride on Dal Lake

Arrive at Srinagar Airport, where our representative will greet you. Transfer to a luxurious houseboat on Dal Lake. In the evening, enjoy a Shikara ride while witnessing the sunset over the lake. Stroll through the local market before an overnight stay in the houseboat.

Day2

Srinagar Sightseeing – Mughal Gardens & Local Markets

After breakfast, explore the famous Mughal Gardens – Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh, and Chashme Shahi. Visit Shankaracharya Temple for a panoramic view of the city. In the evening, shop for Kashmiri handicrafts, Pashmina shawls, and saffron at Lal Chowk Market. Overnight stay in Srinagar.

Day3

Excursion to Sonmarg – The Meadow of Gold

After breakfast, take a scenic drive to Sonmarg, famous for its glaciers and lush green meadows. Enjoy optional activities like horse riding and a trek to Thajiwas Glacier. Return to Srinagar in the evening for an overnight stay.

Day4

Srinagar to Pahalgam – Valleys & River Lidder

After breakfast, drive to Pahalgam, also known as the Valley of Shepherds. En route, visit Avantipur Ruins and Saffron Fields. Upon arrival, enjoy the beautiful scenery of Lidder River and explore Betaab Valley, Aru Valley, and Baisaran Hills (Mini Switzerland). Stay overnight in Pahalgam.

Day5

Pahalgam Leisure & Adventure Activities

Spend the day at leisure in Pahalgam. Enjoy activities like river rafting in Lidder River, horse riding, or a nature walk through pine forests. Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day6

Pahalgam to Gulmarg – Snow Adventures & Gondola Ride

After breakfast, drive to Gulmarg, a paradise for adventure lovers. Enjoy Gondola Ride (Asia’s highest cable car) to Apharwat Peak, offering breathtaking snow-clad views. Try skiing, snowboarding, or simply play in the snow. Stay overnight in Gulmarg.

Day7

Gulmarg to Srinagar – Leisure & Shopping

After breakfast, return to Srinagar. Spend the day at leisure, relaxing or shopping for souvenirs, dry fruits, carpets, and Kashmiri jewelry. Enjoy a traditional Wazwan dinner before an overnight stay in Srinagar.

Day8

Departure from Srinagar

After breakfast, check out and transfer to Srinagar Airport for your return journey, concluding your Kashmir getaway.
