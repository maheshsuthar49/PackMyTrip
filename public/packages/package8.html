Itinerary

Day1

Arrival in Paro – Transfer to Thimphu

Arrive at Paro International Airport, where our representative will greet you. Drive to Thimphu, the capital of Bhutan, passing through scenic valleys and rivers. Check into your hotel and visit the Tashichho Dzong, a stunning fortress that serves as the government’s administrative center. Spend the evening exploring local markets before an overnight stay in Thimphu.

Day2

Thimphu Sightseeing

After breakfast, visit the iconic Buddha Dordenma Statue, offering panoramic views of the city. Explore the National Memorial Chorten, the Changangkha Lhakhang Monastery, and the Motithang Takin Preserve to see Bhutan’s national animal. Later, visit the Simply Bhutan Museum to experience Bhutanese culture before an overnight stay.

Day3

Thimphu to Punakha (75 km / 3 hrs)

Drive to Punakha, crossing the scenic Dochula Pass, where you can see the 108 memorial chortens and breathtaking Himalayan peaks. Visit the magnificent Punakha Dzong, one of Bhutan’s most beautiful fortresses, located at the confluence of the Mo and Pho Rivers. Later, take a short hike to Chimi Lhakhang, the ‘Temple of Fertility.’ Stay overnight in Punakha.

Day4

Punakha to Phobjikha Valley (80 km / 3 hrs)

After breakfast, head to the stunning Phobjikha Valley, home to the rare black-necked cranes. Visit the serene Gangtey Monastery and enjoy a nature walk through the valley’s picturesque landscapes. Spend the evening soaking in the peaceful atmosphere before an overnight stay in Phobjikha.

Day5

Phobjikha to Paro (180 km / 6 hrs)

Depart for Paro, enjoying the scenic drive through Bhutan’s beautiful countryside. En route, stop at Rinpung Dzong, a beautiful fortress with spectacular architecture. Visit the National Museum of Bhutan to learn about the country’s rich history before an overnight stay in Paro.

Day6

Hike to Tiger’s Nest Monastery

Early in the morning, embark on a thrilling hike to Tiger’s Nest Monastery (Taktsang Monastery), Bhutan’s most iconic landmark, perched on a cliffside. The 4-5 hour trek offers breathtaking views of the valley below. After descending, relax at a traditional Bhutanese farmhouse with a hot stone bath (optional). Stay overnight in Paro.

Day7

Chele La Pass Excursion

Take a scenic drive to Chele La Pass, one of Bhutan’s highest motorable roads, offering panoramic views of the Himalayan range. Visit the Kila Gompa Nunnery, one of Bhutan’s oldest nunneries, before returning to Paro for some leisure time and shopping. Overnight stay in Paro.

Day8

Departure from Paro

After breakfast, check out and proceed to Paro International Airport for your onward journey, concluding your magical Bhutan experience.
