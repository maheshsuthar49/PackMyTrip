Itinerary

Day1

Arrival in Port Blair – Cellular Jail & Light and Sound Show

Arrive at Veer Savarkar International Airport in Port Blair, where our representative will greet you. Check into your hotel and relax. In the evening, visit the Cellular Jail, a historic colonial-era prison, and witness the Light and Sound Show, narrating the struggles of Indian freedom fighters. Stay overnight in Port Blair.

Day2

Port Blair to Havelock Island – Radhanagar Beach

After breakfast, board a ferry to Havelock Island, famous for its stunning beaches. Check into your resort and later visit Radhanagar Beach, ranked among the world’s best beaches. Enjoy the sunset and relax before an overnight stay in Havelock.

Day3

Elephant Beach Excursion & Water Sports

Start your day with a boat ride to Elephant Beach, known for its crystal-clear waters and vibrant coral reefs. Enjoy snorkeling, jet skiing, and other water activities (optional). Spend time relaxing on the beach before returning to your resort. Stay overnight in Havelock.

Day4

Havelock to Neil Island – Natural Bridge & Laxmanpur Beach

Check out from your hotel and take a ferry to Neil Island, a serene destination with untouched beaches. Visit the famous Natural Bridge, a rock formation shaped by sea waves. Later, unwind at Laxmanpur Beach, known for its golden sunsets. Stay overnight in Neil Island.

Day5

Bharatpur Beach & Return to Port Blair

After breakfast, visit Bharatpur Beach, perfect for swimming and glass-bottom boat rides. Later, board a ferry back to Port Blair and check into your hotel. Spend the evening at leisure or explore local markets. Stay overnight in Port Blair.

Day6

Ross Island & North Bay Island Excursion

Take a boat trip to Ross Island, once the British administrative headquarters, now home to ancient ruins and deer roaming freely. Continue to North Bay Island, where you can enjoy scuba diving, snorkeling, and a glass-bottom boat ride to explore coral reefs. Return to Port Blair for an overnight stay.

Day7

Departure from Port Blair

After breakfast, check out and proceed to the airport for your return flight, concluding your scenic Andaman adventure.
