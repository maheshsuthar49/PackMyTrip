Itinerary

Day1

Arrival in Delhi

Upon arrival at Delhi, meeting and assistance followed by transfer to hotel. Overnight stay at hotel.

Day2

Explore Delhi

Breakfast at the hotel and enjoy the full day sightseeing of New Delhi in the morning, including Laxmi Narayan Temple, India Gate, Parliament House, and more. Overnight at the hotel.

Day3

Delhi to Samode

Breakfast at the hotel and drive to Samode. On arrival at Samode, enjoy sightseeing and a unique cultural experience.

Day4

Jaipur Tour

Explore the forts and palaces of Jaipur, including Amer Fort, City Palace, and Hawa Mahal. Enjoy a local cultural evening program.

Day5

Jodhpur Exploration

Drive to Jodhpur and visit Mehrangarh Fort, Umaid Bhawan Palace, and Jaswant Thada. Stay overnight at the hotel.

Day6

Udaipur Sightseeing

Travel to Udaipur and enjoy sightseeing, including Lake Pichola, City Palace, and Saheliyon-ki-Bari. Overnight at the hotel.

Day7

Departure from Udaipur

Conclude your trip with a morning walk around Udaipur markets and transfer to the airport for your departure.
